Good — now you are at the real core of the project: choosing the scheduling algorithm.

This is not a simple SQL problem anymore. Your system is a classic:

Constraint Satisfaction Problem (CSP)

because you must assign:
(Class, Subject, Teacher) → (TimeSlot)
while respecting multiple hard constraints.

🧠 Best algorithm for your case
✅ 1. Backtracking (CSP Backtracking Search) — BEST CHOICE
✔ Why this is the correct choice

Your problem has:

many constraints (teacher, class, availability, hours)
no single mathematical formula solution
combinations explode quickly

So you need:

🔁 Try → Check constraints → If fail → go back

That is exactly Backtracking.

📌 What Backtracking does in your system

It builds the schedule step by step:

pick a Class
pick a Subject (that still needs hours)
pick a Teacher (who can teach it)
pick a TimeSlot
check ALL constraints
If valid → place it
If invalid → try another option
If nothing works → backtrack
🧩 Why this fits your problem perfectly

Your constraints:

Class conflict ❌
Teacher conflict ❌
Availability ❌
Subject hours exact ❌
Teacher max hours ❌

👉 These are hard constraints → perfect for CSP backtracking

⚠️ Problem with brute force

If you try all combinations:

Classes × Subjects × Teachers × TimeSlots

This explodes exponentially.

So brute force is impossible.

🚀 2. Improved Backtracking (RECOMMENDED VERSION)

You should NOT use plain backtracking.

Use:

⭐ Backtracking + Heuristics (Smart CSP)
🔥 Heuristic 1: Minimum Remaining Value (MRV)

👉 Choose the hardest thing first:

class with least flexibility
subject with highest required hours
🔥 Heuristic 2: Most Constrained Teacher First

Pick teacher with:

fewer available slots
fewer subjects
🔥 Heuristic 3: Forward Checking

Before placing a lesson:

👉 check if future schedule will become impossible
(if yes → reject immediately)

🧠 Why this is important

Without heuristics:

algorithm becomes slow
may loop forever
may fail on full schedule

With heuristics:

faster
smarter
finds solution in realistic time
