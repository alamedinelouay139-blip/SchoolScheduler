4) VERY IMPORTANT constraints (you enforce in logic)

Database alone is NOT enough. Your algorithm must ensure:

✔ No class conflict
No duplicate (ClassID, TimeSlotID)
✔ No teacher conflict
No duplicate (TeacherID, TimeSlotID)
✔ Teacher availability
Check TeacherAvailability before insert
✔ Teacher max hours
COUNT(Schedule WHERE TeacherID) <= 25
✔ Daily break

👉 For each teacher per day:

Max 5 slots per day
✔ Subject hours
COUNT per (ClassID + SubjectID) = RequiredHours
